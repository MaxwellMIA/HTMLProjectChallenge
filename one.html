Roblox is an online gaming platform where people can make there own games or play other peoples games. Roblox is extremely popular with kids and adelts alike, having many people of differant age, race, gender, and more playing there game everyday. Roblox is available to anyone that has a electronic device with Wifi, which makes its widely available to people. Roblox is used for playing playing games and making friends.

It is free to sign up to Roblox, but there is in-game currency called robux. One USD dollar is equal to 80 robux.
